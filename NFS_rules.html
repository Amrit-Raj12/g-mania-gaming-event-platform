NFS LAN [Rules And Regulations]

NFS MW Competition Rules

Game Version: NFS Most Wanted 1.3
General Competition Method: 4 players

Rules For Preliminaries:

  • Game Type: Circuit, Sprint
  • The Circuit is for 2 rounds 
  • Players getting the 1st & 2nd postion shall qualify for the next round.


Rules for the tournament:

  1. The tounament will be knock out.
  2. The player who wins their 1st stage match advances to the next stage.
  3. There will not be any match played for the 3rd spot.


Game Settings

  1. Courses: Course selection is done by the Game Administrator
  2. Only Bonus Cars can be used (BMW MGT3 can be used ony in finals)
  3. Any type of trainer or any type of software(patch\crack) are not allowed.
  4. Players are provided with a standard keyboard and mouse. They can also use controller if they want.


Penalty for Unfair Play

  • Use of any cheat program like:- trainer or any type of software(patch\crack)
  • Intentional disconnection
  • Use of any settings exceeding standard and permitted sttting
  • If the match is disrupted due to unnecessary chatting, the player may be given a warning or lose by default at the referee's sole discretion.
  • Upon discovery of any player commiting any violations regarded as unfair play, that player will be disqualified from the Tournament/Preliminaries.
  • During the course of any match, the operations staff and/or referee may determine other actions to be unfair play at any time.
