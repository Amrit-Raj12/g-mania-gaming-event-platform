ABOUT US

G-Mania is an eSports gaming tournament platform organised by CSE Department of Quantum University, Roorkee. eSports or Competitive Gaming Tournaments are played across platforms such as PC, Console & Mobile. We currently have 2 verticals - LAN Tournaments & an Online eSports Portal. LAN Tournaments are held on Computer Lab. Online eSports Portal was launched in Aug 2017 with an idea of bringing the tournaments experience online & with a vision to reach out to every gamer. With a passion and a commitment to the best gaming experience, we at G-Mania, have strived to create a community for gamers.

Quantum University unfolds a whole new age of learning! It takes into account your aspirations and your passions. It offers a unique inter-disciplinary environment where one could study computer science with economics, agriculture with entrepreneurship, or MBA with cyber security. Hundreds of such combinations are waiting to be explored. At the same time one is given the opportunity to follow his or her Passion in Theatre, Sports, Music, Dance, Photography and much more.
